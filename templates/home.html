The Problem

No direct data exists.
We build the signal.

Official datasets on confirmed animal abandonment do not exist in Colombia. PetWatch addresses this gap by engineering a proxy risk signal from indirect institutional indicators — abuse reports, veterinary emergencies, microchip coverage, and adoption rates.

The system targets animal protection entities and local authorities across Bogotá and Cali, providing a data-driven lens to prioritize intervention zones before abandonment escalates.

Higher abuse rate correlates with higher abandonment risk
Lower microchip coverage indicates less responsible ownership
More veterinary emergencies reflect greater animal vulnerability
Low institutional presence increases territorial risk
