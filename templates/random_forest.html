Algorithm Details

How the model works

Random Forest builds {{ n_estimators }} independent decision trees, each trained on a random subset of the data and features. The final prediction is the majority vote across all trees — reducing overfitting and increasing robustness compared to a single tree.

The target variable (risk_label) was engineered from a composite risk score: score = maltrato×0.4 + urgencias×0.35 + aprehendidos×0.15 + (1/microchip)×0.1. Localities in the top third are labeled HIGH, middle third MEDIUM, bottom third LOW.
